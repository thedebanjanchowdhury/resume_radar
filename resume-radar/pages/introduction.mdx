---
title: Resume Radar API
---

Screen your resume against JD with AI-powered scoring.

## What it offers

- AI-powered resume screener leveraging Open Source Large Language Models.
- Get HR level summary & first impression of your CV.
- Analyse resume score against a given job description.

## Configuration

You can find the full configuration for your Zudoku site in the `zudoku.config.jsx` file.

For further information on how to customize your site, check out the
[Zudoku documentation](https://zudoku.dev/docs).
